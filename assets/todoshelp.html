Photo Vault
(The ToDo List)

Photo Vault is a very unique app as it allows users to Hide Photos that you don't want others to see from your standard Gallery application. It's unique because it is also a fully functioning ToDo List that as a bonus, also has a fully functioning "Hidden" ToDo list.

If someone does get a hold of your phone and accidentally finds the Hidden area, you can simply tell them that it is a Hidden ToDo list and show them by entering your Hidden ToDo PIN, all the while they have no idea that it is really hiding a "Hidden" Gallery.

Another indispensable feature is the "Quick Escape". If you are looking at your Hidden Gallery and someone approaches and you need to get out fast and you do not want to make it obvious by shaking your phone, simply click the Volume rocker to quickly change the image to either the Default Quick Escape image or one of your choosing.

How to use...
 

Press the + to add a New ToDo List item

To delete an item, Press & Hold the item. You will be asked "Delete Item?" Press it to delete the selected item.

 

 
To access the hidden area, Press and Hold the ToDo List icon, depending on which PIN is entered, it will take you to the appropriate area.
If you enter your Hidden ToDo PIN, the Hidden ToDo List will open.

You could use this area to store important information such as Site Passwords, PIN numbers, or kind of information you choose.

 
You can also access the Hidden Gallery from this window as well. Just Press and Hold the ToDo List icon, and again, depending on which PIN is entered, it will take you to the appropriate area.
Once in the Hidden Gallery you can view your images.

To add a new item, press the + and choose your Standard Gallery from the list.

Choose the image you wish to add.

 
The image will be transferred to your Hidden Gallery and removed from your Standard Gallery.

 

To restore an image (put it back into the Standard Gallery) tap the Restore button. You will be asked to Confirm that you really want to put it back.

 

To delete an image, tap the Delete button. You will be asked to Confirm that you really want to delete it. Remember, you moved it out of your Standard Gallery, so if you delete it here, it's gone forever.

The Menu options are...

Change the Gallery PIN

Change the Hidden ToDo List PIN

Set your own QucikEscape Image
